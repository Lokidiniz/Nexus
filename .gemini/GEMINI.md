## Research Decision System (RDS)

Apply before any creation task:
- **REUSE** (≥90%) → existing approach works unchanged
- **ADAPT** (60–89%) → modify existing for your context
- **CREATE** (<60%) → new approach, requires explicit justification

## Constitution

Non-negotiable: no fabricated references, no invented data, researcher autonomy preserved, limitations always declared, full reproducibility.

See `.nexus/constitution.md` for full principles.
